AI Governance

Prepare your company for AI ethics and governance audits

EU AI Act — In force August 2026

We help companies anywhere in the world navigate agentic AI compliance — where multiple agents make autonomous decisions with real impact on people, operations, and finances.

01

EU AI Act Readiness

High-risk AI system assessment, technical documentation, and transparency registries. Audit preparation under the EU AI Act (in force August 2026). Applicable to any company operating in the EU or with European customers.

02

Agentic AI Compliance

Multi-agent ecosystem audits. Traceability of autonomous decisions (who decided what, and why). Human-in-the-loop protocols for critical decisions. Agent governance: roles, permissions, and action limits.

03

AI Risk Assessment

Risk identification in deployed or in-development AI systems. Bias, discrimination, and opacity evaluation. Frameworks: ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act. Risk mapping by level: minimal, limited, high, unacceptable.

04

Ethics by Design

Ethical principles incorporated from system design. Internal responsible AI policies. Team training on agentic governance. Board-ready evidence for directors and investors.

Blue Peaks practices what it preaches. VIA Connect is built with governance by design: multi-tenant RLS, append-only audit ledger with SHA-256 hash chain, human-in-the-loop on Document Intelligence, and full traceability of every payment and commission decision.
